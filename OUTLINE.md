# Outline: Strategic Communication Issue Brief

## My Three Main Points
1. Communication in transportation planning doesn’t focus on children.
2. Children are the most vulnerable and critical population to plan for.
3. The lack of mobility freedom for children negatively impacts adults.

## Use the SMART Technique
1. Story: tell a story that is relevant and engaging
2. Main Idea: do not bury the lead. Immediately follow the story with the main idea.
3. Agenda: explain what you will cover in your report
4. Reasons: expansion of the agenda. The first heading does not come until after the agenda. If you have subpoints, you'll need to give a subagenda for each main point. Concrete examples and illustrations. Transitions help readers connect ideas. Use headings as sign posts.
Task: Wrap everything up, restating the main idea, and make the call to action.
